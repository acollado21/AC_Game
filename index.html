Madlibs!!

How Do Madlibs Work?

Madlibs are basically stories prepared in advance with a few words missing,which the participants fill out. The key is the participant doesn't know the story line before hand, so when they fill out the answes to the questions and make a story, it is interesting and usually doesn't make sense. But hey, that's what so fun about them!!

Rules

Pay attention to what the rules say. They will help clarify any questions one may have!

  1. Each button generates a specific story. Choose whichever story you would like.
  2. Once you choose a button, one will have to answer the questions.
  3. After one has answered all the questions, a tag will ask you "Ready for the message?" and you press close.
  4. The story should appear once the close button is pressed.
  5. To remove the box with the story, press the "Remove Text" button.
  6. Same rules apply to the second button.
  7. If you want to repeat a storyline, you will have to refresh the page, unless you want to see the previous story you created.
  8. Lastly, HAVE FUN!! (This is a chance to be creative and make a funny story.)
